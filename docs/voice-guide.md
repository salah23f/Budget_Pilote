# Flyeas Voice Guide

*A concierge, not a chatbot. Confident, specific, warm — never breathless.*

---

## The five principles

1. **Specific over smart.** Use real numbers. "We watched 4,812 flights for you this week" beats "AI-powered price tracking".
2. **Warm but grown-up.** We're a first-class lounge, not a birthday party. No exclamation marks, no 🎉.
3. **Zero AI-speak.** If a line needs the word "AI" to impress, the line is weak. Describe what we *do*, not what we *are*.
4. **Short, declarative, active voice.** "You save $142 on this route" — not "$142 in savings can be earned on this route."
5. **Icons, never emojis.** Lucide icons, custom SVG. Emojis in text feel like a boomer CEO's linkedin post.

---

## Forbidden words and phrases

| Forbidden | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Smart *(anything)* | Tech-bro cliché |
| AI-powered / Powered by AI | Tell us what it does, not what it runs on |
| Revolutionary | Only governments are |
| Unleash | No one "unleashes" travel |
| Simplified | We're not a clock radio |
| Supercharge | Marketing spam |
| The world's first… | Usually false, always weak |
| Get started today! | Spammy. "Begin" is enough. |
| Let's go! | Children say this |
| Oops! / Whoops! | We're not a toddler |
| 🎉 ✈️ 🌟 😍 in body copy | Visual noise |

## Rules per UI state

**Empty states** — acknowledge, suggest, never apologize.
> "No bookings yet. Your first mission will show here when it closes."

**Loading states** — one sentence max, no ellipsis abuse.
> "One moment." · "Checking prices." · "Almost there."

**Error states** — own it, suggest the next step.
> "That didn't load. Try again, or check your connection."

**Success states** — acknowledge, point forward.
> "Mission created. We'll alert you the moment a fare matches."

**CTAs** — verb + object. Never "Click here".
> "Begin" · "Start Pro trial" · "Plan a Lisbon trip" · "Redeem 500 points"

**Metadata / timestamps** — lowercase, short, no "ago" fluff where possible.
> "2h" · "yesterday" · "Dec 14"

## Copy rhythm

- **Hero headline**: 3-8 words, no period. Serif.
- **Hero subhead**: one sentence, ≤ 18 words. Sans.
- **Section eyebrow**: 1-3 words, UPPERCASE micro.
- **Section title**: 3-10 words, serif or sans.
- **Body paragraph**: 1-3 sentences, ≤ 60 words. Never more than one paragraph per idea.
- **Card title**: 1-4 words.
- **Card body**: ≤ 14 words.
- **Button label**: 1-3 words.

---

## Tone by context

- **Marketing / landing** — confident, editorial, a little wry.
- **In-app** — precise, factual, helpful.
- **Error / warning** — calm, owning it, actionable.
- **Rewards / celebration** — proud but restrained. No confetti bombing.
- **Emails** — feels like a personal note, not a newsletter.
